Pyramid for Timetrackers


Pyramid is the operational monitoring tool for GBIS projects. It enables, among other things, the management of resources, project planning and monitoring of activities.


The main objective if to get a consolidated view of current and forecast resources, by team, RC, ITEC unit, by organization axis and or geographical location.


For you, timetrackers, the aims is to time-track each week in order to get consolidated data, to track progress on activities and inform the accounting and ppm tracking tools of the days consumed on the activities.
